  • Bis bald, Fahrrad #2

    was the final project for the class Collective Methods


    It documents my time in Berlin, and the stories that happened

    as I explored the city by bike. I picked out five places, and placed

    my photos and stories along photos that other people taken

    at these spots on instagram to form a collective narrative.


    Living and biking in Berlin was a really interesting experience because

    you could really explore the city's complicated history through its

    incredibly vibrant streetscape.


    Browse the pages to view my stories alongside the stories of others.

    Uses Html, CSS, and Javascript.
