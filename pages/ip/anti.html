  • Anti-Social Anonymous

    was the final project for the class Digital Fabrication.


    I liked the idea of creating geometric models of things from a 3d model,

    and decided to create large domes for when you would like some personal

    space. The panels were generated with software, cut by laser cutter, and

    pieced together by hand. A small screw was 3D printed to connect the top

    of the dome to a long piece of string.


    Stick head inside dome to experience.

    Uses Rhino, Meshlab, 123D Make, 3D Printing, and paper.
