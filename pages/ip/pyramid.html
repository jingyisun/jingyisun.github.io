  • Light Pyramid

    was the final project for the class Network Everything.


    I wanted to make a simple light that can be "wirelessly" controlled.

    I soldered four leds onto a perfboard, and attached them to a Uno.

    The microcontroller receives signals from the computer via bluetooth.

    Outer shell of the light was designed in Illustrator, tested with cardboard

    and finally, laser cut in white acrylic.


    Toggle control panels to remotely change the color of the light.

    Uses Arduino, Processing, bluetooth, and laser cutting.
