  • Bubbles!

    was the final project for the class Intro to Computational Media


    This was the first project I ever completed at IMA.

    It was my first attempt at creative coding, and also

    a first try at connecting physical components with

    virtual ones.


    Colorful "bubbles" appear on the screen when you

    blow on the pressure sensor, and the harder the force,

    the faster the speed at which the bubbles float upwards.


    Blow on the bubble wand to create virtual bubbles on screen.

    Uses Arduino and Processing.
