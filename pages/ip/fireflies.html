  • Fireflies

    was the final project for the class Nature of Code.


    This project consists of two parts: the fireflies, and the forest. Fireflies

    are generated using a flocking system, and are random in size and

    postion. The forest consists of five layers of recursive trees, random in

    position, and different in size and color to simulate depth.


    Open p5.js sketch to watch simulation.
